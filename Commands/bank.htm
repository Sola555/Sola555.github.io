Bank

The bank is a place where you can store your coins. Every day you will get 1% more of the coins you have. This can become an incredible coin feeder. It is recommended that you put almost all your coins in the bank and leave it for a few days or even a year to have so much more coins in it another time you look.
Goto command
,goto b
Command Name Command Variables Command Description Command Requirement
,deposit amount of money you want to deposit to the bank Deposit money to your bank. More coins than the given amount. Must be at the bank to use this command as well as commands bellow.
,withdraw Amount of money you want to withdraw from the bank. Withdraw money from the bank into your wallet. More than the given amount of coins.
,bankinfo !!OPTIONAL!! Mention user to get information of them. If this arguement is left blank, by default the user will become you. Look at how many coins are in a user's bank. None except for being at the bank.
